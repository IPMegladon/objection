Command: ios keychain remove

Usage: ios keychain remove --account <account> --service <service>

Removes an entry from the iOS keychain. Both account and service parameters
are required to identify which keychain entry to remove.

Flags:
   --account   The account name (required)
   --service   The service name (required)

Examples:
   ios keychain remove --account user@example.com --service com.example.app
